About me - Simon van Meegdenburg

My name is Simon van Meegdenburg and I'm a data passionate. Ever since I was a kid, I've been crazy about figures, statistics and analyzing stuff. I remember as the day of yesterday that my mother made an excel sheet for me so that I could keep track of the standings of the Formula 1 World Championship. I could stare at it for hours and fantasize about how my favorite driver would have to perform in order to win the championship.

We are a few years ahead in time and I can now spent hours experimenting with an API filled with data for all events in the history of Formula 1. So it's no wonder that besides data passionate I can now also call myself a data professional. I like to spend my time learning new programming tricks or watching clips about the latest trends in the world of data.

This website was a result of me learning my first steps in webdevelopment back in 2019. Another thing I did because I enjoy creating things with computers. I also find it a great way to show more of myself to the outside world. Here you find everything I think you should know about me. You can read about my working experience and the knowledge I gained through different studies, books and courses. Also you can read about my career switch back in 2018. Besides data, I'm also interested in all things around personal growth. You'll find blogposts I wrote back in 2015/16 about some social experiments I tried. Those were originally written in my native language and are now translated to English. You can find all of this under the 'content' button on the top-right of this page. The knowledge section also contains some hints about my interest in personal growth.
